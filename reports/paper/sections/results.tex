On an earlier 800-blend normal held-out evaluation, Thayer-Direct improves
affected-region MSE from 0.062555 for identity to 0.004428, a 14.13x
improvement. On the current 1,000-blend comparable normal evaluation,
Thayer-Direct affected-region MSE is 0.004236, Thayer-Residual affected-region
MSE is 0.004431, and Thayer-BR v0.1 affected-region MSE is 0.002451.

On the hard stress test, Thayer-Direct affected-region MSE is 0.009390,
Thayer-Residual affected-region MSE is 0.007069, and Thayer-BR v0.1
affected-region MSE is 0.004587. Thayer-BR v0.1 reduces worse-than-identity
stress cases to 0/1000, compared with 13/1000 for Thayer-Direct.
