# Held-deblender-family evaluation

Status: **UNRESOLVED** in Thayer-Audit v0.

Condition C was the only family with an immutable promptable checkpoint,
aligned train/validation/calibration outputs, and a source-group-excluded
training subset under the audit contract. R0 and R1 belong to the same compact
prompted-U-Net family cluster and cannot create family diversity. Thayer-PU is
a stochastic candidate family, but it did not provide complete aligned
out-of-fit requested-source outputs under one frozen deployment sampling rule.
The prompted ResUNet failed its prompt-swap admission gate.

No leave-one-family-out rotation was run, family identity never entered an
auditor input, and no deblender-agnostic claim is made. The separate held-family
strong-pass gates therefore remain untested.

The one next experiment should first establish at least one physically
compliant, structurally distinct frozen family with complete aligned
source-group-safe outputs. A later audit must freeze its family rotations,
models, calibration, and thresholds before held-family evaluation.
