# Family-E1 label support

Family-E1 v0 produced zero safety labels. The unchanged Thayer-Audit gates were
not applied because prompt identity failed before checkpoint freeze and
inference. Safe/unsafe counts, prevalence, source-group support, catastrophic
pass, joint-safe rate, family distinctness, and bootstrap intervals are all
unmeasured—not zero-valued scientific estimates.

The physical outputs evaluated during preflight and micro fitting were finite,
nonnegative for both catalog sources, and conservative within tolerance. That
physical result does not satisfy the label-support gates. Thayer-Audit v1
remains unauthorized, and no auditor was trained.
