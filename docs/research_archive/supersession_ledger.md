classification, executor, or interpretation; the predecessor remains valid

## Non-superseding coexistence rules

- A valid negative result remains evidence even when a later correction repairs
  its implementation prerequisite.
- A local identifiability result cannot supersede a global multi-start result.
- A conditioning improvement cannot be silently translated into source
  recovery.
- A simulation-contract proof cannot be promoted to real-survey generality.
- Empty support is not uniqueness.
- Fail-closed engineering runs may explain why science did not run, but they do
  not supply scientific outcomes.
